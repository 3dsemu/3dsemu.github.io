About 3DSemu

3DSemu is one of the few Nintendo 3DS emulators. It was previously open-source, but

it is now closed-source. A small team of talented developers are now releasing updates

to 3DSemu. The emulator can run both homebrew and commercial games at full speed. There

have been no reports of crashes so far. 3DSemu also has actual 3D support and WiFi support

Best of all, 3DSemu is free! Yep, this emulator is 100% free!
